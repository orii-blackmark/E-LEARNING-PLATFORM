Help Center

Welcome to the help center for our e-learning platform. Here you can find answers to common questions and get help with any issues you may be experiencing.

FAQ

  • How do I enroll in a course?

    To enroll in a course, go to the Courses page and browse the available courses. Click on the course you want to enroll in and follow the prompts to complete the enrollment process.

  • How do I reset my password?

    To reset your password, go to the Settings page and click on the "Change Password" button. Follow the prompts to reset your password.

  • How do I view my progress in a course?

    To view your progress in a course, go to the My Progress page. Here you can see which lessons you've completed and how well you've done on any quizzes or assessments.
